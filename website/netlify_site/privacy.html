Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At HelloBrick, we prioritize the protection of your digital and physical collection data. This policy explains our practices.

1. Data Collection

We collect only the minimum data required to enable cloud-sync for your brick vault and account settings (email). We do not sell or share your collection data with third parties.

2. Camera Access

Camera permission is used solely for real-time brick scanning. Images are processed on-device whenever possible. We do not store private photos or videos beyond the anonymized metadata required to improve our scanning algorithms.

3. Account Deletion

You have the absolute right to delete your data. Initiating the "Delete Account" process in the app settings will permanently purge all your cloud records within 24 hours.

4. Contact Us

For questions regarding your privacy, contact info@keydesignmedia.xyz.
