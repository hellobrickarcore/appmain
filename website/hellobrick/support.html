How do I scan my bricks?

Open the HelloBrick app and navigate to the Scanner tab. Point your camera at a flat surface with your bricks. For best results, ensure good lighting and separate the bricks so they don't overlap too much.

Is HelloBrick free?

HelloBrick offers a free tier with basic scanning features. Premium subscribers unlock advanced part identification, unlimited virtual bins, and exclusive building ideas.

What bricks are supported?

We currently support most standard building bricks, including traditional plates, bricks, and specialized pieces. We are constantly updating our AI models to include more categories like Technic and Minifigures.
