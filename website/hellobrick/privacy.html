Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 18, 2026

HelloBrick ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data to ensure a smooth and secure experience.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best AI scanning experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your name and email address provided during sign-up.
  • Image Data: Images captured via the scanner are processed in real-time to identify bricks. We do not store these images permanently on our servers unless you explicitly choose to contribute them to our training database.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with the app, including scan frequency and collection size.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the HelloBrick service.
  • Improve our AI detection models through anonymized metadata.
  • Communicate with you regarding account updates or support requests.

3. AI Processing & Third Parties

We use Gemini AI for advanced part recognition. Anonymized scan metadata may be processed by Google Cloud services. We ensure all third-party integrations comply with strict data protection standards. Your identifiable data is never sold to third parties.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can export your collection or delete your account at any time through the Profile settings in the app. Upon deletion, all personal data is purged from our systems within 30 days.
