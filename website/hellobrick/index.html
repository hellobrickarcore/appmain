Frequently Asked Questions

How does the HelloBrick scanner work?

HelloBrick uses state-of-the-art computer vision and custom-trained neural networks to recognize over 45,000 unique brick geometries in real-time using your smartphone camera.

Is HelloBrick compatible with LEGO bricks?

Yes, HelloBrick is designed to identify and catalog most standard building bricks, including those from major brands like LEGO. We are an independent platform and not affiliated with the LEGO Group.

Can I see what I can build with my bricks?

Absolutely! Once you scan your bricks into your digital vault, HelloBrick's Idea Engine suggests models and MOCs (My Own Creations) that you can build with your existing parts.
