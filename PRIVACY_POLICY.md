# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

HelloBrick ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Account information (username, email)
- Profile information and preferences
- User-generated content (brick scans, creations, posts)
- Training data contributions (for model improvement)

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device information (model, OS version)
- Usage data (features used, time spent)
- Camera data (only when actively scanning)
- Location data (if enabled, for regional features)

## How We Use Your Information

- Provide and improve our services
- Train and improve our AI models (with your consent)
- Personalize your experience
- Send notifications (with your permission)
- Analyze usage patterns
- Ensure app security and prevent fraud

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data:
- With your explicit consent
- For AI model training (anonymized)
- To comply with legal obligations
- With service providers (under strict confidentiality)

## Your Privacy Rights

- **Access**: View your personal data
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request account deletion
- **Privacy Settings**: Control account visibility (public/private)
- **Data Export**: Request a copy of your data

## Account Privacy

- **Public Accounts**: Your profile and creations are visible to all users
- **Private Accounts**: Only you can see your profile and creations
- You can change this setting anytime in Profile > Settings

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encrypted data transmission
- Secure storage
- Regular security audits
- Access controls

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.

## Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests:
- Email: info@keydesignmedia.xyz
- In-app: Profile > Settings > Privacy Policy

## Compliance

- **iOS**: Complies with Apple App Store guidelines and iOS privacy requirements
- **Android**: Complies with Google Play Store policies and Android privacy requirements
- **GDPR**: Complies with EU General Data Protection Regulation
- **CCPA**: Complies with California Consumer Privacy Act

## Camera and Media Access

- Camera access is required for brick scanning
- Images are processed locally when possible
- Scanned images may be used for AI training (with consent)
- You can revoke camera access in device settings

## AI Model Training

- Your contributions help improve detection accuracy
- Training data is anonymized
- You can opt-out of contributing training data
- Your personal information is never used in training
