# HelloBrick Design Review

## 🎨 Current Design Overview

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Orange (#FF6B35 / orange-500) to Yellow (#FDB813 / yellow-500) gradients
- **Background**: Soft orange/yellow gradient (orange-50 to yellow-50)
- **Cards**: White with orange-100 borders
- **Accents**: Orange-400, Yellow-400, Orange-600

### Typography
- **Font**: Nunito (friendly, rounded)
- **Headings**: Bold, slate-900
- **Body**: Regular, slate-600
- **Sizes**: Responsive (text-xl to text-3xl)

### Components

#### 1. Header
- ✅ White background with backdrop blur
- ✅ BrickBuddy mascot (🧱 emoji) with mood states
- ✅ Daily streak badge (orange gradient)
- ✅ Profile button
- ✅ Level & XP display

#### 2. Home Tab
- ✅ Daily Quest card (orange gradient, prominent)
- ✅ "Start a Brick Quest" CTA card
- ✅ Progress cards (2-column grid)
- ✅ Daily Goals list with checkboxes

#### 3. Scan Tab
- ✅ Camera view with rounded corners
- ✅ Quest progress overlay (when active)
- ✅ Detection results overlay
- ✅ Large scan button (orange gradient)
- ✅ Results cards (2-column grid)

#### 4. Quests Tab
- ✅ Quest cards with icons
- ✅ Progress bars for color quests
- ✅ Difficulty stars
- ✅ XP rewards
- ✅ Daily quest highlighting

#### 5. Profile Tab
- ✅ Profile card with avatar
- ✅ Stats grid (2x2)
- ✅ Daily streak visualization
- ✅ Settings toggles
- ✅ Badges section

#### 6. Onboarding
- ✅ 4-step flow
- ✅ Large emoji icons
- ✅ Skip button
- ✅ Progress dots

### Design Strengths
✅ Consistent orange/yellow color scheme
✅ Rounded corners (rounded-2xl, rounded-3xl)
✅ Good use of shadows and depth
✅ Smooth transitions and hover effects
✅ Mobile-friendly spacing

### Areas for Enhancement
🔧 More consistent spacing system
🔧 Better visual hierarchy
🔧 Enhanced micro-interactions
🔧 More polished button styles
🔧 Better empty states
🔧 Improved loading states
