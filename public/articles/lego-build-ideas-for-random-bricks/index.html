5 Lego Build Ideas for Your Messy Legos Pile

Don't let your bulk Legos gather dust. Even if you don't have enough pieces for a massive castle, you can still create something incredible. Here are 5 lego build ideas for your random brick collection.

1. Micro-Cities

Micro-scale building is perfect for random Legos because you only need a few parts for each building. Use 1x1 plates for windows and slopes for roofs. A handful of bricks can become an entire skyline.

2. Desk Organizers

Lego builds can be functional! Build a phone stand, a pencil holder, or a cable organizer. The modular nature of Legos lets you customize the height and width to fit your exact desk setup.

3. Modular Animals

Use your hinges, clips, and ball joints to create poseable creatures. Mixing colors can actually make "alien" lego builds look more interesting and organic.

4. Marble Mazes

Create a 2D labyrinth on a large baseplate using 1-stud wide bricks for walls. This is a great lego build idea to use up long 1x4 and 1x6 bricks that aren't useful elsewhere.

5. Geometric Art

Flatten your bricks! Use plates and tiles on a baseplate to create "pixel art" or abstract mosaics. If you hate to sort lego, this is the best way to use up those thousands of tiny 1x1 plates.
